Adoption Types

Types of Dogs
Type Description
Papillon A steadfast companion. Has a friendly personality that can steal your heart.
Greyhound Quite, well mannered and independent. Doesn't thrive amid yelling and tension
Basset Hound Solemn, not prone to dramatic displays of affection. Has acutely developed practical skills
Alaskan Malamute Independent, inquisitive and resourceful. Adapted to unforgiving environments.
Golden Retriever Insightful, easy going and fun to have around. Great with kids and kind to stranger.
Boxer Bold, exuberant, affectionate, self confident and utterly loyal. Patient with family.
German Shepherd Strong work ethic. Prized for sound judge, strength and protective instincts.


Types of Cats
Type Description
Persian A long-haired breed of cat characterized by its round face and short muzzle. It is also known
as the "Persian Longhair" in the English-speaking countries.
Maine Coon It has a distinctive physical appearance and valuable hunting skills.
Siamese One of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Asian cat. Derived from the Wichianmat
landrace, one of several varieties of cat native to Thailand
Ragdoll A cat breed with a color point coat and blue eyes.
Bengal Domesticated cat breed created from hybrids of domestic cats, especially the spotted Egyptian
Mau, with the Asian leopard cat.
Burmese A breed of domestic cat, originating in Burma, believed to have its roots near the Thai-Burma
border and developed in the United States and Britain.
